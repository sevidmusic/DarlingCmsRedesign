Get Out The Vote Video Contest

What we are looking for?

30 - 90 second videos that will inspire people to vote!

Create a video to share with others about voting, post it on Youtube, send us the URL, and, if appropriate* we will post it on the site, for others to view and share.

The video can be about the history of black voting, current events, you and people you know talking about voting or the issues you care about. You can record yourself singing a song you have written, or reading a poem. You can play the audio over photographs or clips.
