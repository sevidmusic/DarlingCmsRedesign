“Get Out The Vote Video Contest”
What we are looking for? Videos that will inspire people to vote! How the contest works:
  1. Create a 30 - 90 second video encouraging voting
  2. Put credits at the end of the video
  3. Post it on YouTube
  4. Submit the video's YouTube url at https://blackballotpowercontest.org
  5. If appropriate we will post it on our website for others to view
 The most popular videos will be judged by entertainment professionals and 3 winners will be announced on Sept 15. We will send Press Releases to the media and promote the winners on social media sites. The videos can be about the history of black voting, current events, you and people you know talking about voting or the issues you care about. You can record yourself singing a song or reading a poem you have written. You can play the audio over photographs or clips. Whatever you want. We want everyone to vote. But our focus is on connecting and communicating with Black Voters. Videos must speak to our community Begin accepting submissions: Aug. 6th Deadline for submission: Sept. 10th For more information and to submit your video go to:

https://blackballotpowercontest.org/ * Note: We are a not-for-profit, nonpartisan organization. To maintain our tax status we cannot endorse a candidate or party. We are encouraging people to vote.
